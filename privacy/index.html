Privacy Policy

Summit • Effective March 2026

Summit is an outdoor conditions app providing weather, tides, avalanche, snowpack, and related data for outdoor activities. This policy explains what data is handled when you use the app.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. The developer has no access to your data.

All data stored by the app remains on your device, including:

  • Recent location searches
  • Selected activity mode
  • Cached conditions data for offline use

Third-Party Services

To provide conditions data, the app communicates with the following third-party services. Your GPS coordinates and/or search queries are sent to these services as part of normal app function. Summit has no control over how these services handle your data. Please review their individual privacy policies.

  • Open-Meteo - weather and forecast data. Your coordinates are sent to retrieve local conditions. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source service. Privacy info
  • Nominatim / OpenStreetMap - location search and geocoding. When you search for a location by name, your query is sent to Nominatim's servers. OpenStreetMap Foundation Privacy Policy
  • USGS National Map (EPQS) - elevation data. Your coordinates are sent to the USGS public elevation API. This is a public government service.
  • NOAA Tides and Currents - tide predictions. Your coordinates are used to find nearby tide stations via NOAA's public API. This is a public government service.
  • Avalanche.org - avalanche danger ratings. Your coordinates are used client-side to match against public avalanche forecast regions. Avalanche.org Privacy Policy
  • NRCS AWDB (SNOTEL) - snowpack data. Your coordinates are used to find nearby SNOTEL stations via the USDA Natural Resources Conservation Service public API. This is a public government service.

Location Data

The app requests access to your device's location to automatically load conditions for your current position. Location data is used only to fetch conditions data from the third-party services listed above and is never stored on external servers by us. You can deny location access and search for locations manually.

Data Retention and Deletion

Recent searches and cached conditions data are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data is never transmitted to the developer. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data permanently.

Children

Summit is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
